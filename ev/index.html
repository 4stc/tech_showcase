Cars aren't the only EVs

While there is a lot that could be said about electric cars, I'm far more interested in the advancement of the other kinds of EVs, such as:

Core Advancements

  • Cost

    Electricity is fairly cheap and the price doesn't fluctuate nearly as much as something like gas

  • Less Risk

    EV injuries which don't involve cars are usually not overly serious

  • Accessibility

    Easier to use than cars and no license or training is required

Challenges to Overcome

  • Lack of Adoption

    Most towns/cities were designed specifically for cars, which means using non-car EVs can be tricky in some situations

  • Short Distance Focus

    Currently electric cars are the only EVs designed for long distance travel. There are some very powerful electric bikes, but even are lacking for long distance travel
