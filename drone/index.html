Core Advancements

  • Versatility

    Drones can be used for so many things, such as: delivery, military operations, photography, mapping out unexplored areas, farming, or just as a fun hobby. I've even heard of drones being used to put out fires

  • Speed

    Drones can easily cover long distances in a very short amount of time

Challenges to Overcome

  • Weather

    All-weather drones are difficult to build and often can't withstand extreme weather conditions

  • Maintenance

    Drones have many complex parts, these often need to be fixed or replaced
